What is Horned Sungem?

With Plug and AI in mind, HS is dedicated to be the simplest and wieldest AI device to let all developers, students, AI hobbyist and lovers create their AI applications with ease. Without any dependency on deep learning frameworks or complex libraries, just plug HS into the USB port and run a short installation script, your device will be ready to see and understand the world.

Technology advantages of HS

Through a unique and integrated front-end design withe Intel Movidius MA245X VPU, HS is able to achieve high performance with low power consumption (<3W). HS develop toolkit is based on Python so its simple to use, support most platforms - MacOS, Linux (Ubuntu, Raspbian), Android, Windows (release soon), and most importantly, it’s open-sourced. Moreover, developers can train their own model through popular frameworks and convert to HS-format to replace built-in models.

Ecosystem

In the future, we will put our effort on improving the SDK, building an actived society and an online model store that encourage users to share or trade their models. HS is hoping to serve anyone that’s willing to experience, create AI applications and ultimately, solve the troublesome real-life problems.
